Treats Your Dog Will Go Crazy For: Healthy and Tasty Options

Let’s face it—dogs are pretty much all about the snacks. But finding treats that are both tasty and healthy can be like finding the Holy Grail of pet food. Fear not! I’ve rounded up the top dog treats that will keep your dog’s tail wagging and your conscience clean. From grain-free to meaty morsels, here’s a list that’s as irresistible as it is nutritious!

Quick Summary of Our Top Picks:

  1. Zuke’s Mini Naturals – Low-Cal Treats for Training
  2. Greenies Dental Dog Treats – For Clean Teeth and Fresh Breath
  3. Blue Buffalo Wilderness Trail Treats – Meat-Lover’s Dream
  4. Fruitables Crunchy Dog Treats – Fruity Fun in Every Bite
  5. Wellness CORE Pure Rewards – Limited Ingredient Treats for Sensitive Tummies
  6. SmartBones Peanut Butter Chews – Rawhide-Free Chew Treat
  7. Old Mother Hubbard Classic Dog Biscuits – Classic Crunch with Healthy Ingredients
  8. Rocco & Roxie Jerky Sticks – Soft, Savory Jerky Sticks

1. Zuke’s Mini Naturals – Low-Cal Treats for Training

These tiny treats are packed with flavor, low in calories, and perfect for training sessions. They come in several flavors, including chicken, peanut butter, and salmon, so there’s bound to be something for even the pickiest pups.

Pros

  • Low-calorie: Only 3 calories per treat.
  • Small size: Perfect for training.
  • Lots of flavors: Keep your dog’s taste buds guessing!

Cons

  • Soft texture: May not be for crunch-loving dogs.
  • Could dry out: Keep sealed for freshness.

Rankings

  • Taste Appeal: 10/10
  • Healthiness: 9/10
  • Value: 8/10

Our Take

These are the go-to treats for training. Not too rich, just the right size, and a flavor profile that’ll keep your dog laser-focused on you (or, realistically, the treats).

  • Freshens breath: No more stinky kisses.
  • Variety of sizes: Perfect for any breed.

Cons

  • Pricier: Premium dental treats come with a cost.
  • Not super long-lasting: Fast chewers may finish them quickly.

Rankings

  • Taste Appeal: 8/10

  • Value: 7/10

Our Take

If you’re looking for a treat that doubles as a tooth-cleaner, Greenies are an excellent choice. Plus, they might even make those post-treat kisses a little more bearable.
